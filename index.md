## What Is Poji?

Poji is a local web manga reader for your own CBZ files. It is meant to make a folder of manga volumes feel like a simple personal reading library without turning into a heavy media server.

This wiki is intentionally small for now. Start with [About Poji](/about), then skim [Getting Started](/getting-started) and [Features](/features).
